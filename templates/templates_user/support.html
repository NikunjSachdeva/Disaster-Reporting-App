Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a disaster?

You can report a disaster by clicking on the "Report Disaster" button on the main dashboard. Fill in the necessary details and submit.

How can I view reported disasters?

Go to the "View Reports" section to see all the reported disasters in your area.

What kind of disasters can I report?

You can report any disasters such as floods, earthquakes, road blockages, and any other emergency conditions affecting your area.
